# Accessibility

This doc describes accessibility expectations for important product actions and public surfaces.

## Contract

- Policy documents and examples express infrastructure governance meaning in language platform and security teams can review.
- Decision reasons work in the SaaS UI, GitHub/GitLab feedback, and evidence exports.
- Risk summaries are concise, action-oriented, and understandable without color, icon, or layout context.
- Downstream renderers can present severity, decision effect, rule ID, and reason as text fields so GitHub/GitLab feedback, SaaS UI, and exported evidence remain readable in assistive technologies.
- Reason text identifies the risky infrastructure change and expected reviewer action without requiring readers to inspect raw templates or plans first.
